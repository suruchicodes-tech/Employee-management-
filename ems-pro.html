FAQ

Questions people actually ask

Straight answers to the things most teams want to know before getting started.

How does role-based access work?

Admins can view, create, edit, and delete all records across all modules. Employees can view their own profile, apply for leaves, and check their attendance and salary slip. Role permissions are managed from the Users panel.

Can I export salary and attendance reports?

Yes. All reports can be exported to PDF or Excel. The salary report includes gross pay, deductions, and net pay per employee. Attendance reports can be filtered by month, department, or individual employee.

Is employee data stored securely?

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. The system supports session expiry and audit logs so you can track who changed what and when.

How many employees can the system handle?

The system is built on a relational database and scales to tens of thousands of employee records. Performance is optimised with indexed queries so search and reporting remain fast at any size.

What leave types are supported?

Out of the box: Casual, Sick, Earned, Maternity, and Paternity leave. Custom leave types can be added from the admin panel. Leave balances and carry-forward rules are configurable per type.

Can employees mark their own attendance?

Yes. With employee-role access enabled, staff can self-check-in and check-out via the portal. Admins can review and override any entry. WFH, half-day, and on-site statuses are all supported.
